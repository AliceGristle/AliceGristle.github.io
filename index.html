The Game

Lady of the Dark Lake is a videogame under development. Intended as an homage to the NES classic Final Fantasy II, it is, like its object of adoration, an adventure role-playing game designed in 8-bit style.
Other things that inspired Queen of the Dark Lake include Irish and Germanic legends, Wagner's operas, Tsaichovsky, Dark Souls, swans, ravens, and the more desperate parts of Fire Emblem: Genealogy of Holy War.
Currently, the game is in the planning stage. I've drawn some pictures and written some of the story, but mostly I just look at Hans Holbein pics and recreate clavier sounds in FamiTracker. Expect a year or more for a playable demo!
