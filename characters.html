Characters

The last of the Lake Knights of Findael, the Knight comes to Findael's aid at her darkest hour. Deadly and indomitable, the Knight is prepared to fight the immense armies of the Emperor till the last drop of blood.
Fair Anna is the last Queen of Findael. She is of ancient Eladi blood, knowledgeable in magic and wise in the ways of the Goddess. Though desperate to protect her people, she is young and untried, and many fear her courage will fail in the face of this ungodly enemy - the Empire of Isidor, which seeks the destruction of all the Eladi.
Cunning as a rat, deft as a snake, Gormlei serves Queen Anna faithfully as her envoy and diplomat - or spy, as some say. Though her sharp tongue lashes both friend and foe, no one is more devoted to fair Anna than Gormlei.
Fiall the Old sits at Anna's table, advising her in matters of tradition. Fiall knows the legends2 concerning Findael, Ardail, and Osgiel, the great cities of the Eladi, and much ancient lore besides. However, these turbulent times, where hell-wind blows and armies march, are not for the frail old Fiall. He fears his time will soon come...
The youngest of the royal huntsmen of Findael, Palendil is one of the few of his brotherhood who survived the fall of Findael. Now he sits at Anna's table, advising her in matters of war. In his daydreams, he is a mighty warrior and the lover of the Queen, but when dreams fade, is his sword-arm as strong as his fancy?
Simeona, Queen Anna's royal magician, is descended from the most ancient of Eladi families. Her knowledge of magic comes from distant Muiren, yet even her powers did not avail against the Emperor's horrible sorcery. Now, Simeona waits grudgingly for the final attack, and seeks in vain for a way to help her people one last time.
Master of magic, commander of the great armies of the north, the Emperor is bent on destroying the beautiful cities of the Eladi. Nothing can stand in the way of his ambition - neither Anna's subtle spells nor the Knight's stalwart sword. Both the mist-shrouded west and the sun-washed east will fall under his rule in the end.
