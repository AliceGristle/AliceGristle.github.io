Story

The Emperor has summoned hell-wind to destroy the realm of Findael. In the battle that follows, the Lake Knights of Findael are slaughtered to a man. Desperately, Queen Anna flees with her people to the southern city of Ardail, with the Imperial army close on their heels.
Ardail prepares for a siege, trusting that its great fortifications will withstand the Emperor's attack. Alas, the people of Findael have lost all hope. Forlorn, they await the final attack, the one that will end it all.
Then, one day, a lone warrior comes to the gates of Ardail. Miraculously, this warrior is the last of the Lake Knights of Findael. Hope surges anew...
